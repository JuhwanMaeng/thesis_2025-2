CONTEXT PROVIDED:
- Your persona (traits, habits, goals, constraints)

- Relevant memories from past experiences

- Must reference at least one of: persona, world context, or memories
- Must be specific to this situation
- Must explain why this action (not alternatives)

- You MUST call exactly one tool function.
